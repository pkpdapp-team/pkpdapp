This is the PKPDApp, a web-based application to model the distribution and effects of drugs.

The PKPDApp is an open-source project that provides tools for modelling drug distribution and effects. To ease collaborations for drug discovery, the app puts a huge emphasis on making modelling as transparent, shareable and reproducible as possible.

Explore Data

Visualise your population data. Stratify by dose, covariates or individual IDs, and more...

Simulate

Build and compare models to explore dosing regimens and effect variations between individuals.

Infer Model

Find the model that describes your data. Compare the identifiability of models and explore the potential for optimised experiments.
