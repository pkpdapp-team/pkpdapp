Simulate

This is the workflow to explore the behaviour of PKPD models away from data. It's purpose is to provide an easy interface to compare different dosing regimens, model parameters or even model structures.

Step 1: Build Model

A model is composed of a mechanistic model, a route of administration of the drug, an error model and a population model. For further details on the definition and usage of the terms, please have a look at the documentation.

Mechanistic Model

Model the pharmacokinetic and pharmacodynamic processes.

Route of Administration
